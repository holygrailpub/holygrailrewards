Holy Grail Rewards Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Usage of Personal Information.
We collect personal information upon enrollment into the Holy Grail Rewards Program. We collect:
 • First and last name
 • Email
 • Mobile phone number


We use this information for personalization and communication purposes. This information will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

Device Information.
We collect information from or about the computers, phones, or other devices where you install or access our Services, depending on the permissions you’ve granted. We may associate the information we collect from your different devices, which helps us provide consistent Services across your devices. Here are some examples of the information we collect:
 • Attributes such as the operating system, hardware version, device settings, file and software names and types, battery and signal strength, and device identifiers.
 • Device locations, including specific geographic locations, such as through GPS, Bluetooth, or WiFi signals.
 • Connection information such as the name of your mobile operator or ISP, browser type, language and time zone, mobile phone number and IP address.

Geolocation Data.
The application uses geolocation data to provide our services. When you use our application, we may use GPS, Bluetooth , WiFi or other technologies to determine your current location. Verification of your location is required for check-ins to earn Rewards. We will not share your current location with other partners. If you do not want us to use your location for the purpose identified above, you should turn off the “location services” feature in the app located within your mobile phone’s settings. You may revoke your consent for access to geolocation data at any time, but the check-in feature to earn rewards will not work without geolocation verification.

Cookies Usage.
Holy Grail's Reward Program uses cookies and similar technologies (“cookies”) on our websites to facilitate and improve your online experience. Cookies contain small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit our website. Cookies are sent back to our website to allow us to recognize your device. Unless you disable the cookies on our website, by using our website you agree to our use of those cookies.
